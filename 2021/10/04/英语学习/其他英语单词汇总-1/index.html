其他英语单词汇总-1

Posted on 2021-10-04 | In 英语学习 | | Visitors:
生活中遇到的不会的单词汇总

Unit1 Psychology

New words

  • axiomatic：
    adj.
    something that is axiomatic does not need to be proved because you can easily see that it is true.
    不需证明的，不言自明的
  • blurry：
    adj.
    indistinct or hazy in outline
    模糊的
  • forge：
    vt.
    to develop something new, especially a strong relationship with other people, groups, or countries.
    形成，缔造
  • crucible：
    a container in which substances are heated to very high temperatures
    坩埚，熔炉
  • inform：
    vt.
    • to officially tell someone about something or give them information.
    • to influence someone’s attitude or opinion
      影响 (某人的态度或观点)
  • entangle：
    vt.
    • to make something become twisted and caught in a rope, net etc
    • to involve someone in an argument, a relationship, or a situation that is difficult to escape from
      使卷入，使陷入
  • disconcerting：
    adj
    Making you feel slightly confused, embarrassed, or worried.
    令人困惑的，令人尴尬的，令人不安的
  • contour：
    n
    • the shape of the outer edges of something such as an area of land or someone’s body.
      轮廓，外形
    • (contour line) a line on a map that shows points that are of equal heights above sea level
  • discern：
    vt.
    • to notice or understand something by thinking about it carefully.
      (仔细想过之后) 察觉出，弄清楚，辨明
    • to be able to see something by looking carefully.
  • fickle：
    adj.
    • someone who is fickle is always changing their mind about people or things that they like, so that you cannot depend on them ——used to show disapproval
      三心二意的，靠不住的，变化无常的
    • Something such as weather that is fickle often changes suddenly.
      (天气等) 变幻莫测的
  • erratic：
    adj.
    something that is erratic does not follow any pattern or plan but happens in a way that is not regular.
    不规则的，不确定的，不稳定的
  • contemplative：
    adj.
    Spending a lot of time thinking seriously and quietly
    沉思的，冥想的，默想的
  • confines：
    n.
    Within / beyond the confines of something
    范围，界限
  • inscribe：
    vt.
    to carefully cut, print, or write words on something, especially on the surface of a stone or coin.
    (尤指在石头或硬币表面) 雕刻，印制，题写
  • purport：
    vt.
    to claim to be or do something, even if this is not true.
    声称
  • bystander：
    n.
    Someone who watches what is happening without taking part.
    旁观者，局外人，看热闹的人
  • dilute：
    vt.
    • to make a liquid weaker by adding water or another liquid
    • to make a quality, belief etc weaker or less effective.
      降低 (质量)；削弱 (信念)
  • reconcile：
    vt.
    if you reconcile two ideas, situations, or facts, you find a way in which they can both be true or acceptable.
    使和谐一致；调和；协调
  • luminous：
    adj.
    • shining in the dark
      发光的；夜明的
    • very brightly coloured, especially in green, pink, or yellow.
  • crystallise：
    v.
    • if a liquid crystallizes, it forms crystals
    • if an idea, plan etc crystallizes or is crystallized, it becomes very clear in your mind.
      (使想法、计划等) 变得明朗而具体
  • vivify：
    vt.
    to give new life or energy to something.
    使生动，使活跃
  • solitary：
    adj.
    • used to emphasize that there is only one of something.
    • Doing something without anyone else with you
      单独的，无伴的
    • Spending a lot of time alone, usually because you like being alone
  • introspection：
    n.
    the process of thinking deeply about your own thoughts, feelings or behaviour
    内省，反省
  • insomnia：
    n.
    if you suffer from insomnia, you are not able to sleep
    失眠

  • consummate：
    adj.

    • showing a lot of skill
    • used to emphasize how bad someone or something is.

    vt.

    • to make a marriage or relationship complete by having sex
    • to make something complete, especially an agreement
      实现，完成
  • embody：
    vt.
    • To be a very good example of an idea or quality
      代表，体现 (思想或品质)
    • to include something


其他不会的单词

  • scaffold：
    n.
    • a structure built next to a wall, for workers to stand on while they build, repair, or paint a building
      建在墙边的结构，供工人在建造、修理、粉刷建筑物时站立
    • a raised structure which was used in the past as a place to kill criminals by hanging them or cutting off their heads.
    • (American English) a structure that can be moved up and down to help people work on high buildings.
  • insurance：
    n.
    • an arrangement with a company in which you pay them money, especially regularly, and they pay the costs if something bad happens, for example if you become ill or your car is damaged
    • the business of providing insurance
    • the money that you pay regularly to an insurance company
  • insurance policy / policies：
    保险政策
  • insurance company：
    保险公司
  • cyber：
    网络
  • cyber-
    prefix
    Relating to computers, especially to messages and information on the Internet.
  • coverage
    n.
    • when a subject or event is reported on television or radio, or in newspapers
    • the protection an insurance company gives you, so that it pays you money if you are injured, something is stolen etc
    • the range of subjects and facts included in a book, programme, class etc
    • when something affects or covers a particular area or group of things
  • grocer：
    n.
    someone who owns or works in a shop that sells food and other things used in the home
  • grocery：
    n.
    a supermarket
  • modest：
    adj.
    • someone who is modest does not want to talk about their abilities or achievements
    • not very great, big, or expensive
    • shy about showing your body or attracting sexual interest, because you are easily embarrassed
    • modest clothing covers the body in a way that does not attract sexual interest
  • chatbot：
    n.
    a computer program designed to send messages back to a human user, as if it is having a conversation
  • seamless：
    adj.
    done or made so smoothly that you cannot tell where one thing stops and another begins
  • asymmetrical
    adj.
    having two sides that are different in shape
  • hindquarters
    n.
    the back part of an animal with four legs
  • privacy
    n.
    • the state of being able to be alone, and not seen or heard by other people
    • the state of being free from public attention
  • memoir
    n.
    a short piece of writing about a person or place that you knew well, or an event that you experienced
  • sentimental：
    adj.
    • someone who is sentimental is easily affected by emotions such as love, sympathy, sadness etc, often in a way that seems silly to other people
    • based on or relating to your feelings rather than on practical reasons
    • a story, film, book etc that is sentimental deals with emotions such as love and sadness, sometimes in a way that seems silly and insincere
  • supervisor
    n.
    • someone who supervises a person or activity
    • someone who is a member of the city, county etc government in some parts of the US
  • reflections：
    n.
    • an image that you can see in a mirror, glass, or water
    • careful thought, or an idea or opinion based on this
    • something that shows what something else is like, or that is a sign of a particular situation reflection of
    • the action or process of light, heat, or sound being thrown back from a surface
  • essentially：
    adv.
    used when stating the most basic facts about something
  • self-contained
    adj.
    • complete and not needing other things or help from somewhere else to work
    • someone who is self-contained does not seem to need other people or show their feelings
    • a self-contained apartment has its own kitchen and bathroom
  • self-sufficient
    adj.
    able to provide all the things you need without help from other people
  • inherently
    adv.
    天生地
  • rational
    adj.
    • rational thoughts, decisions etc are based on reasons rather than emotions
    • a rational person is able to think calmly and sensibly
    • able to make sensible judgments
  • skepticism or scepticism
    n.
    an attitude of doubting that particular claims or statements are true or that something will happen
  • decree
    n.
    • an official order or decision, especially one made by the ruler of a country
    • a judgment in a court of law
      v.
      to make an official judgment or give an official order
  • permanent
    adj.
    continuing to exist for a long time or for all the time in the future
  • beneath
    adv.、prep.
    • in or to a lower position than something, or directly under something
    • covered by something
    • used to say that someone’s real character or feelings are not shown because their appearance or behaviour is different
    • not good enough or suitable for someone
    • in a lower, less important rank or job than someone else
  • trade-off
    n.
    a balance between two opposing things, that you are willing to accept in order to achieve something
  • philosophy
    n.
    • the study of the nature and meaning of existence, truth, good and evil etc
    • the views of a particular philosopher or group of philosophers
    • the attitude or set of ideas that guides the behaviour of a person or organization
  • enquiry / inquiry
    n.
    • a question you ask in order to get information
    • the act or process of asking questions in order to get information
    • an official process to find out about something
  • co-evolve
    共同发展，共同进化
  • evolve
    v.
    • if an animal or plant evolves, it changes gradually over a long period of time
    • to develop and change gradually over a long period of time
  • presume
    v.
    • to think that something is true, although you are not certain
    • to accept something as true until it is shown to not be true, especially in law
    • to behave without respect or politeness by doing something that you have no right to do
  • domestic
    adj.
    • relating to or happening in one particular country and not involving any other countries
      国内的
    • relating to family relationships and life at home
    • used in people’s homes
  • dispute
    n.
    a serious argument or disagreement
  • against
    prep.
    • used to say that someone opposes or disagrees with something
    • used to say that an action is not wanted or approved of by someone
    • used to say that something is not allowed by a law or rule
    • used to say who someone is competing with or trying to defeat in a game, battle etc
  • articulate
    v.
    • to express your ideas or feelings in words
    • to pronounce what you are saying in a clear and careful way
    • if something such as a bone in your body is articulated to another thing, it is joined to it in a way that allows movement
  • emphasise
    v.
    • to say something in a strong way
      强调
    • to say a word or phrase louder or higher than others to give it more importance
    • to make something more noticeable
  • deprived
    adj.
    not having the things that are necessary for a comfortable or happy life
    被剥夺的
  • sustain
    v.
    • to make something continue to exist or happen for a period of time
    • to suffer damage, an injury, or loss of money
    • if food or drink sustains a person, animal, or plant, it makes them able to continue living
    • to make someone feel strong and hopeful
    • to hold up the weight of something
  • coherent
    adj.
    • if a piece of writing, set of ideas etc is coherent, it is easy to understand because it is clear and reasonable
    • if someone is coherent, they are talking in a way that is clear and easy to understand
    • if a group is coherent, its members are connected or united because they share common aims, qualities, or beliefs
  • whereas
    • used to say that although something is true of one thing, it is not true of another
    • used at the beginning of an official document to mean ‘because of a particular fact
